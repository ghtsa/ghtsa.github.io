July 1995

First UAV

The GA MQ-1 Predator Unmanned Aerial Vehicle is put to the test in the field. Developed by the US military, it was a major innovation in future ar technology. UNtil the commercialization of personal ars, the Predator ar would be the cover model of UAVs and the image most people would conjure when hearing the word “ar”.

2007

ars in police work

A UAV operated by police in Washington successfully located a man wanted by the police. The police after searching and chasing after a man, deployed a ar in order to improve search efforts. One of the first successful UAV searches employed by police.

January 2010

Consumer ars

The first smartphone-controlled quadcopter, the Parrot AR ar, is introduced at the Consumer Electronics Show by the French ar company Parrot. It won several technology and innovation awards and was praised for its ease of use and smartphone compatibility.

December 1, 2013

Amazon Prime Air

Amazon unveils a futuristic way of delivery called Prime Air where packages are delivered through the use of specialized ars.These ars will allow delivery to individuals in less than 30 minutes, something that has previously been unprecedented and unheard of.

December, 14, 2015

FAA Regulations

The FAA requires that all small ars weighing between 0.55 pounds and 55 pounds must be registered with the FAA. While some were thankful and others outraged, this registration was a big step towards ar safety and regulation.
